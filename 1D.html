About the Band

One Direction is a British-Irish boy band formed in 2010. The group consists of Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, and formerly Zayn Malik. They became one of the world's biggest bands with hits like "What Makes You Beautiful" and "Story of My Life."
